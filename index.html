Our Energy Solutions

Solar Installation

The advantages of using solar cells as source of electrical energy far outweigh most other energy sources. It is a reneweable form of energy that is clean and relatively cheap. We install solar panels on houses roof tops, street light pole stands and other heights safely and secured.

Inverter Installation

When choosing a power inverter, it is important to choose the right size and product. It is also important to access the number of electrical outlets that will be powered and the current draw of each equipment. We provide technical assessment for your inverter needs and install quality products following engineering best practices

Central Solar Water Heater

The central solar water heater runs fully on solar system, eliminating the need to use electric power for water heating purposes. The water storage tanks retains water for 72hours. It is ideal for homes and hotels

Deep Cycle Batteries Supply

Battery capacity is important because it measures the amount of energy you can store. If you need to power certain appliances over long period of time, you will need more batteries. We supply and install quality deep cycle batteries to to provide sustained electrical energy over a period of time.
